In Absolute Reality


The Supreme Being is larger than we can fathom/conceptualize/imagine, with any kind of mind, here.

The problem being that as soon as we say being or even entity, we, by design, by natural pareidolia cannot help ourselves but see an anthromorphic being.
 
 
So, I, will try to only say All That Is -which is a mathematically translatable concept, too- knowing that this notion will only speak to very, very, very, few...
