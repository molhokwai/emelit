☷ I PRAISE THE ANCESTORS FOR THIS FOOD

- 6258 24 Mekher

I Praise
The First Ancestor, Atoum,
For being First Consciousness
Which I am, which We are,

Wasire, the Father, Nature
For His Generosity,
Aïssata, the Mother,
For her Milk & Tenderness,
Horo, the Son/Sun,
For His Power...

_______________
I praise the Ancestors for their blood in my veins,
Their Intelligence in my brain,
Their Wisdom in my heart.

_______________
I praise the Ancestors for those who Love,

...

_______________
I praise the Ancestors, and I pray for more Strength to dispel all that is not Theirs,
And be on a journey worthy of their Glory.


6528, Mekher 24
