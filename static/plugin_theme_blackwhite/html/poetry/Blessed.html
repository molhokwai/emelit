Blessed

With you, I am blessed

6528, Ka Hr Ka ~20

Moneyless & loosing perfectness
Breaking the curse and getting out' this mess
Winking myself into joyfullness
Musing insensitive, in senselessness


With you, I am blessed
So I really couldn't care more, or less
And it's a fathomless feeling of completeness
