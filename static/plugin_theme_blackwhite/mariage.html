The principles of a marital group community

Who is it for

_______________
For the hyperaffectives, the hypererotics. Meaning the ones that thrive on giving and receiving a lot of affection, and living a rich sensual and sexual life.

Who can handle it ?

_______________
Only those who are truly free. Free from the ideas of possession (possessiveness), exclusivity, and more. 

Why a marital group community instead of classical mono or polygamous relationships?

_______________
Only for those who can handle it: it leads to more stable relationships, to more stability and balance:

  • You have no obligation of being with this one person if/when you don't need or want to, so less to no chances of being frustrated, and more
  • You have many partners to choose from at any moment, so you have from more to all chances of almost always having the right one available
  • And: if you are an hyperaffective/erotic in a classical monogamous relationship, chances are that you already have multiple partners, with all the eventual troubles that that means, and in a polygamous relationship, chances are that you are not or will not remain satisfied
What are the rules ?

_______________
There are no rules, per se, as this is about Freedom. There are only guiding principles:

  • Any member of the community is free to leave at any moment, without having to even justify him or herself
  • Any member of the community is free to have relationships out of the community, but will always share all of the details of those relationships with members of the community
  • Any new member of the community must be accepted by the whole community
  • The maximum size of the community should be kept at or below 12 members
  • There should be an equal number of members of the both sexes, or the number difference between the sexes should not exceed two (2)
Health

_______________
In mono or polygamous relationships as in a marital group community, every person should always care for his/her health, and for the health of his/her partners. So, as a related principle:

  • Every member  as well as any new member of the community should be known and agreed upon to be healthy by all other members, or proven to be healthy to them.
Compatibility

_______________
One enters a marital group community purely by conscious choice, fully knowingly, and leaves the community whenever he or she decides to.

_______________
So, even if compatibility could be an issue in the beginning, it will quickly cease to be, every experience a person has leading him/her to know how and to better choose the right one for him/her.

Standard monogamous relationship

Note: The author, I, have been in standard monogamous faithful (on my side, anyway) relationships for ~20 years, the longest being the one with the mother of two of my two children: ~3 years. So consider this when reading this particular advice, or any other one here.

_______________
If you are into one, or plan to be into one:

  • Be fully conscious and aware of what you are in or are stepping into: don't have any illusion, because you would only be extremely lucky to have those illusions be(come) real
  • Be creative, every day, and make sure that your partner or future partner knows how to be and also is creative. Otherwise you will have to be even smarter for the both of you, which would make things much much more difficult, but not impossible
  • Always keep in mind that there are other options, so as to always know why you are staying or entering in the relationship, so as to always make sure that if/as you decide to stay, you will make the best of it

That's it.

Other non-standard marital structures
The harem

_______________
Reserved for the males who are able to handle it, and have so much Love (care, attention, sensual and affective presence, authority, strength & material posessions... besides sex) to give their women that only a harem would suffice.

The polyandrous relationship

_______________
As for the harem, but for the (truly exceptional) woman and her men.

Men-women, adult|mature relationships

_______________
This all applies to men-women, adult|mature and agreed relationships.

In Conclusion

_______________
The most important is to know what you as a person need, what fits you, what is for you, and what is not. This means knowing exactly and without any of the illusions that infatuation can bring about, what you are stepping into, what you are agreeing to, and thus consciously choosing and being prepared for the love relationship life you will have for the next n years.

Knowing that there are other options helps you be aware, conscious, and hopefully, ready.

Les principes d'une communauté amoureuse

Pour qui

_______________
Pour ceux et celles au développement et à la sensibilité érotiques, affectives et émotionnelles aigüs, les hyperaffectifs/érotiques, la Communauté Amoureuse est un modèle (statistiquement) plus viable, plus équilibré et plus respectueux à long terme que le modèle classique, qu'il soit mono ou polygamique.
La Communauté Amoureuse implique pour chacun de ses membres de la maturité affective, émotionnelle, mentale... donc de la Maîtrise. Lorsque c'est le cas elle offre, au travers de la multiplicité des partenaires, des choix et des expériences la permanence affective, la continuité sensuelle et érotique nécessaire aux hyperaffectifs/érotiques.

Statistiquement plus viable

_______________
... théoriquement, parce qu'atteindre le même niveau de continuité dans un modèle classique implique une compatibilité extrèmement forte entre les partenaires, ce qui par les faits de l'expérience amoureuse d'aujourd'hui, est relativement rare.

Les hyperérotiques/affectifs

_______________
Empiriquement, ils et elles forment la majorité de la société ici au moins, en partie par l'état de tension que cette phase de transition de la Conscience Humaine dans lequel chacun, chacune s'installe, pour cette transition.

Commentaire et Conclusion

_______________
Empiriquement aussi, les personnes ayant vécu jusqu'aujourd'hui dans une communauté amoureuse de fait, à un moment ou à un autre de leur vie et particulièrement ici, sont (largement) majoritaires. Et seules les (anciennes) structures et contraintes sociales et plus, amenant à des impératifs psychologiques restrictifs et inhibitifs, ont maintenu ces communautés amoureuses dans l'ombre, ont fait de leur réalité des sources de déséquilibre, d'écarts de la Vérité, et plus...

L'expansion de la Conscience Humaine mène à cette succession de changements:

  • La Vérité, complète, dans les relations amoureuses, qui les stabilisent et les renforcent, multipliant les couples stables et équilibrés et épanouis ou travaillant à leur épanouissement
  • L'adoption explicite -relativement marginale- de modèles amoureux différents, la Communauté Amoureuse en étant l'un des principaux, sinon le principal

_______________
L'adoption majoritaire de la Communauté Amoureuse est fonction de ce qui résultera de l'Expérience Humaine, fonction du niveau d'hyperéroticité/affectivité du paradigme suivant. Et de la Volonté Générale d'établir des structures relationnelles et sociales qui permettent à/aménent chacun à trouver "la" personne, le ou la partenaire aussi parfaitement compatible que raisonnable.

_______________
Quel que soit le statut du paradigme suivant, la Communauté Amoureuse est le seul modèle vrai qui correspond aux hyperaffectifs/érotiques de nature*, et c'est donc le seul qu'ils adopteront, à terme.

* par contraste aux hyperaffectifs/érotiques qui entrent dans l'état par les circonstances
